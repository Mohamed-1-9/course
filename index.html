What is the difference between the two courses?

The first course focuses on C++ programming, covering the basics and advanced topics like data structures and algorithms. The second course is about front-end web development, starting from HTML and CSS to advanced topics like React and Tailwind.

You can take each level separately.

Details :

These two courses are designed for ages 12 and up to establish a foundation in C++ programming and front-end Web development. The course consists of two weekly sessions: one offline (in-person) and one online. During the online session, students will review the material covered and apply it to small projects to ensure comprehension and practical benefits for all. Assignments will be given, which students must complete and submit before the deadline. These assignments will be reviewed and corrected. All lectures will be recorded so students can revisit them for revision.
